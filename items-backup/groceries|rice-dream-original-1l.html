Have a Rice Dream Day!
A delicious and refreshing non-dairy rice drink!

Making Rice Dream part of your healthy lifestyle could not be easier! The fresh and light taste complements everyone's daily routine. Whether pouring on your breakfast cereal, incorporating in a scrumptious family meal, adding to your pick-me-up cup of coffee or simply used as a refreshing drink, Rice Dream can be enjoyed by all.

Our first and one of our most popular varieties; Rice Dream Original Organic is low in fat and naturally lactose free. Being organically certified means there are no hidden nasties of any kind; just simple ingredients that give a fresh and enjoyable taste.

Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: Microwave

Microwave from frozen. Before cooking: Remove outer packaging and pierce film. Peel back film and remove the Yorkshire Pudding and place aside. Place on a non-metallic plate. During cooking: After 9 minutes (750W) or 8 minutes (850w), remove film over the roast potatoes and add the Yorkshire pudding. Continue to cook. After cooking: Stir thoroughly. Check food is piping hot. Allow to stand for 1 minute. Do not reheat once cooled. All cooking appliances vary. This is a guide only. Microwave on full power. Cat D - 750w. 10 mins. Cat E - 850w. 9 mins.

 

Cooking instructions: Oven

Oven cook from frozen but also can be cooked in the microwave. Before cooking: Preheat oven. Remove outer packaging & pierce film. Peel back film and remove the Yorkshire pudding and place aside. Place on a baking tray on the middle shelf of the oven. During cooking: After 35 minutes, remove film from the roast potatoes, add the Yorkshire Pudding. Continue cooking. After cooking: Check food is piping hot. Allow to stand for 1 minute.All cooking appliances vary. This is a guide only. Oven cook from frozen. 200°C. Fan 180°C. Gas 6. 40 mins.

Preparation

To Serve
Gently shake the bag to coat the fish in the sauce. Cut along the length of the bag with scissors and remove the fillets with a fish slice. Pour the sauce over the fish and serve with stir fry veg or a simple salad.

Oven cook: From Chilled

Remove the bag from the carton. Without opening, carefully separate the fillets thorough the bag and place foil-side down on a baking tray in the middle of a preheated oven. Allow enough room for the bag to double in size during cooking.
Fan oven: 170°C/ Conventional oven: 190°C/Gas mark 5 for 18 mins.
Leave to stand for 1 minute.
Check the fish is piping hot before serving.
Not suitable for microwave cooking.

While we've aimed to remove all the bones, please be careful just in case we've missed one.

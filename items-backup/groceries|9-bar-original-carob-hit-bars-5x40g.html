Fuel your daily adventures and provide sustainable energy to those who really need it... that's good energy, twice!

The Power of Seeds...
9BAR is high in fibre and a source of magnesium, which contributes to a normal energy yielding metabolism and reduces tiredness - that's what we mean when we say 'Good Energy' and why seeds are super.

The Power of Deeds...
'Good Energy, Twice' means... energy for you and energy for others! Every 9Bar we sell = a night of light for a child in Africa. We're working with solaraid to provide solar lights to families who would otherwise rely on dirty, dangerous kerosene lamps.

Thanks for joining our movement...
Let's light up the night, One Bar at a time! 9Bar.com

See the Impact...
Our solar lights mean crucial hours of study for children without access to proper lighting. Trace the impact of your night of light at 9Bar.com/ImpactMap

Preparation

Cooking instructions: General

For best result cook from frozen. All appliances vary; the following are guidelines only.
Check food is piping hot before serving.

Microwave: From Frozen

Remove outer packaging and pierce film lid in several places.
Place on a non-metallic plate. Heat on Full Power for: 750W 4 Mins / 850W 4 mins
Peel back film lid, stir and recover. Heat on Full Power for a further: 750W 3 1/2 Mins / 850W 3 mins
Leave to stand for 2 minutes and stir before serving.

Oven cook: From Frozen

Preheat oven to 190°C/375°F/Gas Mark 5 or Fan Oven 180°C.
Remove from outer packing and pierce film lid in several places.
Place container on a baking tray.
Place near centre of oven or fan oven and cook for 40 minutes.
Allow tray to stand to regain rigidity.
Stir and serve.

WARNING: Although extra care has been taken to remove bones, some small bones may remain.

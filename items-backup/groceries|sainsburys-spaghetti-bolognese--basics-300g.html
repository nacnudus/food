Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: oven

Oven cook from chilled. Electric 190°C. Gas 5. 20 mins. Oven cook from frozen. Electric 190°C. Gas 5. 40 mins. 1 Preheat oven to the required temperature as shown. 2 Remove sleeve and pierce film lid. 3 Place container on a baking tray on the middle shelf of the oven and cook for the required time, halfway through cooking peel back film lid, stir and re-cover. 4 Leave to stand for 1 minute, stir and serve. These are guidelines only as cooking appliances vary in performance. Always check that the product is piping hot before serving.

 

Cooking instructions: microwave

Chilled : Cat D - 750w. 4 mins. Cat E - 850w. 3½ mins. Frozen: Cat D - 750w. 7½ mins. Cat E - 850w. 7 mins. Before Cooking: Remove sleeve and pierce film. Place on a non matallic plate. During Cooking: Halfway through cooking, peel back film, stir and recover. Continue cooking. After Cooking: Check food is piping hot. Allow to stand for 1 minute, do not reheat once cooled

Warnings:

Adjust times according to your particular oven. (For fan assisted ovens cooking times should be reduced. For best results refer to manufacturer's handbook.)
All appliances vary in performance, these are guidelines only.
Microbiological Safety: Always check that the product is piping hot before serving

Not remotely like real fromage frais
It has been made pretty clear in recent years that it's not fat that should be demonised but sugar. This fat free product bears no resemblance to the gorgeous, thick, lactic fromage frais I love to eat in France. It is thin, bordering on watery, and pretty tasteless. If you want a fat-free creamy product then it's far better to go for yoghurt than this.
No, I do not recommend this product.

Absoutely fantastic
I used to love the Sainsbury's basics favoured fromage frais, but for some reason it wasn't available for a few weeks. Lucky me! I tried this and LOVE it, as a diabetic my regular, guilt-fee pudding is some raspberries, kiwi or plums - or a mixture! - with three tablespoons of this on top. I have it every day, it's the best part of any meal. One comment says it's too thin, and there's a little truth in that. When I open a new one I carefully pour away the small amount of liquid on top, and then stir throughly. Problem, such as it is, solved! Excellent product.
Yes, I recommend this product.

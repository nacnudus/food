Description

Apple & Blueberry Flavour - Hot Oat cereal

Helps lower cholesterol

Enjoy as part of a varied and balanced diet and healthy lifestyle. Oat beta-glucan has been shown to lower blood cholesterol. High cholesterol is a risk factor in the development of coronary heart disease. Coronary heart disease has multiple risk factors and altering one of these risk factors may or may not have a beneficial effect.

Oat beta-glucan 2.2g per 100g. Each serving (57g) contains 40% of the 3g of oat beta-glucan suggested per day.

Preparation

Good Morning!
Only 2 mins to perfect porridge...
1 Gently mix dry ingredients with a spoon
2 Add boiling water up to the line on the inside of the pot (170ml)
3 Stir thoroughly straight away, and continue to stir for about 15-30 seconds
4 Place lid back on pot and leave to stand for a further minute
5 Stir again, and enjoy

Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: oven

Oven cook from chilled. Electric 190°C. Fan 170°C. Gas 5. 25 mins. Before Cooking: Preheat oven. Remove sleeve. If cooking from chilled, remove film. If cooking from frozen, pierce film. Place on a baking tray on the middle shelf of the oven. During Cooking: If cooking from frozen, remove film half way through and continue cooking. After Cooking: Check Food is piping hot. Allow to stand for 1 minute. Do not reheat once cooled.

 

Cooking instructions: microwave

Chilled : Cat D - 750w. 5 mins. Cat E - 850w. 4½ mins. Frozen: Cat D - 750w. 8½ mins. Cat E - 850w. 7½ mins. Before Cooking: Remove sleeve and pierce film. Place on a non matallic plate. During Cooking: Halfway through cooking, allow to stand for 1 minute. Continue cooking. After Cooking: Check food is piping hot. Allow to stand for 1 minute, do not reheat once cooled.

Warnings:

Adjust times according to your particular oven. (For fan assisted ovens cooking times should be reduced. For best results refer to manufacturer's handbook.)
All appliances vary in performance, these are guidelines only.
Microbiological Safety: Always check that the product is piping hot before serving

Ingredients

INGREDIENTS:Mussels (Mytilus edulis) (Mollusc) (40%), King prawn (Penaeus vannamei) (Crustacean) (39%), Japanese Flying Squid (Todarodes pacificus) (Mollusc) (20%), Salt.

Mussels caught using dredges in the North east Atlantic Ocean, squid caught using trawls in the eastern Indian or Western Pacific ocean and landed in Thailand, Prawns farmed in Thailand

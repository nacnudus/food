Description

Vegan Pieces are meat free and made with mycoprotein.

Vegetarian Society approved

Quorn Vegan Pieces are low in saturated fat and high in protein. A great tasting, versatile ingredient that the whole family will love.
Alex
Quorn Chef

Mycoprotein is a nutritious form of quality protein, naturally low in fat and high in protein and fibre.

Preparation

Cooking instructions: General

Simply remove all packaging & cook from frozen as follows. For best results, cook on the hob.
All appliances vary, these are guidelines only. Ensure Quorn Vegan Pieces are piping hot before serving.

Microwave: From Frozen

Place Quorn Vegan Pieces in a microwaveable dish, add sauce of your choice, cover with film, pierce & cook on high E/800W: 4 minutes, stir and re-cover, cook for a further 4 minutes. Stand for 1 minute.

Hob: From Frozen

Place Quorn Vegan Pieces and your favourite sauce in a pan. Cover & simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

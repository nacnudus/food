Information

Description

Burgers formed from chopped chicken breast coated in breadcrumbs, prefried.

Made with 100% chicken breast

No artificial colours, flavours or preservatives

Our Chicken Burgers will always be made with 100% chicken breast, using just a few simple ingredients & nothing more.
That's the difference with Birds Eye: You get our finest chicken, tasty & tender, raised responsibly by farmers we know & trust.

Why does it say made with 100% chicken breast if it's 46% chicken breast?
Made with 100% chicken breast means we use only chicken breast meat in our range. The other 54% relates to the other tasty ingredients such as the coating.

Forever Food Together
We are passionate about our sustainability programme to provide you with tasty and nutritious food that is responsibly sourced and prepared - forever

For more visit birdseye.co.uk

Preparation

Cooking instructions: General

To Prepare...
Your Chicken Burgers contain raw meat, please ensure that they are Cooked Thoroughly until piping hot throughout.
Tastiest when Grilled Straight From The Freezer.
Please ensure food is cooked until piping hot.
These instructions are guidelines only.
Do Not refreeze after defrosting.

Grill: From Frozen

Medium Grill 10-12 Mins
Pre-heat grill.
Turn over occasionally.
Cook until crisp and golden.

Oven cook: From Frozen

230°C, Fan 210°C, Gas Mark 8 12 Mins
Pre-heat oven.
Place on baking tray in the middle of the oven.
Cook turning over once until crisp and golden.

Although great care has been taken to remove all bones, some may remain.

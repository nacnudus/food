Overall rating

Some minor flaws, but generally excellent
Appearance
5 out of 5
Quality
5 out of 5
Value
3 out of 5
The filter jug itself is sturdy and well-built, with some neat design touches to make it easy to hold and use. Filtered water capacity is huge, but there are a couple of flaws which make this a less-than-perfect product.
Firstly, the unfiltered water capacity is pretty small. You'll need at least two refills of the top container to provide enough filtered water to fill the main jug - possibly three. Also, you need to be careful filling up the top section, as it will leak out down the handle if you're not careful.
Finally (and I've seen this a lot with various filtered water jugs), there's a lot of small nooks and crannies which are difficult to clean and can allow a build-up of mould if you're not careful, especially around the lid.
Overall, a good product, but has some limitations.

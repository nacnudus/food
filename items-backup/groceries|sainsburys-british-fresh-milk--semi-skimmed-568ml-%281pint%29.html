Support our British farmers!
Unlike the other two reviews, I have been having this milk delivered by Sainsbury's with my shopping order now for about 4 years. I have NEVER had a problem with the milk splitting, with the exception of extremely hot weather in a storm! THAT is a complete mystery to me, as I've been around now for over 60 years and to my memory, all milk in my fridge or larder splits when the weather is stormy and no-one has ever been able to tell me why. This milk is delicious, and I highly recommend it. May I suggest that if you purchase this from a store and carry it home you pack it into a 'cool bag', or take a 'freezy' block with you to pack with the milk. I always did that prior to home delivery, and may I suggest that others try that too? It might just solve the problem of split milk.
Yes, I recommend this product.

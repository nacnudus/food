Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: oven

Oven cook from either chilled or frozen. This product is not suitable for microwave cooking. Before cooking: Preheat oven. Remove all packaging. Place on a wire rack over a baking tray on the middle shelf of the oven. After cooking: Check food is piping hot. All cooking appliances vary. This is a guide only. Oven cook from chilled. 200°C. Fan 180°C. Gas 6. 12 mins. Oven cook from frozen. 200°C. Fan 180°C. Gas 6. 18 mins.

Best Shop-Bought Bhaji
I am a vegetarian and resent the fact that British supermarkets never offer much in the way of choice or quality when it comes to vegetarian items. Even supermarket food based on Asian cuisines offers more choice to chicken-eaters than vegetarians - despite the fact the world's greatest vegetarian food comes from Asia. Crazy or what!?! So it is with special delight that I am able to recommend these bhajis - the best shop-bought bhajis I have ever tasted, miles better than the average bhaji.
Once reheated in the oven, these bhajis have a deliciously crunchy exterior and a sweet, tender inside. The balance of onion to dough is perfect - most shop-bought bhajis are balls of raw onion held together with flavourless paste, nothing you do can make this edible - but here there is less onion so it cooks properly, and the dough is made sweeter and lighter by the addition of sweet potato. Since sweet potato is packed with nutrients, these bhajis are not only tastier but healthier.
I do not normally enjoy sweetness in food but it works here because I eat my bhajis with Patak's hot lime pickle, Sainsbury's popadums, and Sainsbury's minty yoghurt raita and the contrast of flavours and textures is spot-on. Two bhajis makes a snack or light lunch. You just need a simple tomato and green leaves salad, and some bread or rice, your choice of drinks, to make a satisfying main meal. If only Sainsbury's sold gulab jamun or khulfi, I'd have my dream dinner :-)
Two is a serving, eight per pack, makes four meals for less than two quid. They freeze well too. A real bagain.
Warmly recommended!
Yes, I recommend this product.

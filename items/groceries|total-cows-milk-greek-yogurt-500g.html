The best yogurt
We have enjoyed this yogurt for many years. It's real authentic Greek yogurt - creamy, thick and delicious. No other brand comes close. We use it for breakfast almost every day mixed with berries, banana, or pears, topped with granola. Sometimes the water separates from the yogurt but don't worry, just stir it to make it smooth again. I feel the full fat version is healthier and it also tastes better than the 0% version.
Yes, I recommend this product.

Written byAnnette
Posted05 August 2016
Overall rating 
5 out of 5
Closest you can get to real greek yoghurt
I lived in Athens for three years and our local store sold 'village' yoghurt which was always sold in terracotta dishes (non returnable)
I had full fat yoghurt like this every day for breakfast with walnuts and honey or with muselis mixed with Total (no milk but is very filling) and would use it to make the best tsatziki ever to go with salads for dinner.
I lost lots of excess weight because of the fantastically healthy lifestyle. But when we moved to another part of Athens with no store selling the village yoghurt, I had to make do with Total.
There was a difference in taste, and I found Total more creamy and slightly thicker, but that's not a bad thing. I've been eating Total since moving back to the UK and have tried other similar products, but there really is nothing else that can beat it.
A fan of kebabs? You really need to find somewhere that serves Greek souvlaki - as it knocks spots off a kebab!!
My mum buys another stores' own brand of greek style yoghurt, but mum always says Total is still the best as well when I buy Total to take with me (mum is 200 miles away).
Go try it for yourself, you wont be disappointed!
I still have fond memories of going to a daily food market in Greece and finding a Feta cheese seller with 12 different aged feta cheese blocks to try.... bliss!!!
There are many recipes you can use Total with, and don't forget that it is high in protein, so suitable for people wanting to replace animal derived protein now and then.
I've found Total's Facebook page to be very useful for more ideas as well.
So yes, I can heartily recommend Total Greek Yoghurt.
Yes, I recommend this product.

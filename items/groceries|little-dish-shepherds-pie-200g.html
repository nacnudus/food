Shepherd's Pie

British lamb

Every Little Dish pot or pie is a perfectly nutritionally balanced meal for your little one and is approved by our nutritionist - low in salt, no added sugar, at least 1 of 5 a day, a source of protein and balanced for energy and fat. Suitable for 1 year+.

We make our Shepherd's Pie with minced British lamb, tomato and haricot beans in gravy and top it with potato and veg mash.

About Little Dish pots & pies:
When Hillary was expecting her first child, she noticed that no-one was making fresh, proper meals for toddlers that parents could buy in the shops. So she set out to create a range of delicious, healthy meals just like Mums make in their own kitchens using only 100% natural ingredients and no added salt or sugar.

Nutritionist Approved: We work with nutritionist and Mum, Lucy Jones, to ensure that every Little Dish pot or pie is a perfectly nutritionally balanced little meal for toddlers - low in salt, no added sugar, at least 1 of 5 a day, a source of protein and balanced for energy and fat. Visit our website to find out more. And every Little Dish recipe is given the thumbs up by our Tiny Tasters and their Mums before it leaves the kitchen.

Little Dish is proud to have won the coveted Mumsnet Rated badge as well as the Loved by Parents top award for the Best Toddler Food Range for the last 6 years running.

Preparation

Cooking instructions: General

Our Shepherd's Pie is best cooked in the oven if you like a crispy top. Please do not reheat. These are guidelines only. Always test the temperature before serving.

Microwave: From Chilled

1. Take off the card sleeve and pierce the film in a few places.
2. Put on a microwaveable plate and cook for 1 min 30 sec at 800W or 900W.
3. Let your pie stand for 1 min and then cook for another 1 min at 800W or 900W.
4. When it's ready, let it rest for 2 mins in the microwave and then peel off the easy peel film and serve.

Microwave: From Frozen

1. Take off the card sleeve and pierce the film in a few places.
2. Put on a microwaveable plate and cook for 2 min 30 sec at 800W or 900W.
3. Let your pie stand for 1 min and then cook for another 2 min 30 sec at 800W or 2 min at 900W.
4. When it's ready, let it rest for 2 mins in the microwave and then peel off the easy peel film and serve.

Oven cook: From Chilled

1. Preheat your oven to 190°C (electric), 170°C (fan), Mark 5 (Gas).
2. Take off the card sleeve and easy peel film.
3. Put the pie on a baking tray in the middle of the oven for 30 mins.
4. When it's ready, let it rest and cool down for 2 mins before serving.

Oven cook: From Frozen

1. Preheat your oven to 190°C (electric), 170°C (fan), Mark 5 (Gas).
2. Take off the card sleeve and easy peel film.
3. Put the pie on a baking tray in the middle of the oven for 45 mins.
4. When it's ready, let it rest and cool down for 2 mins before serving.

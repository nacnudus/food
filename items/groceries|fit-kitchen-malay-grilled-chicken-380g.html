Preparation

Microwave: From Chilled

1. Remove this label. Open the sauce sachet with scissors and pour the contents into the meal, covering the ingredients.
2. Put the lid back on loosely so steam can escape, then place in a microwave and cook on full power (900W) for 4 minutes.
3. Allow to stand for 1 minute then stir the sauce through the meal. Be careful, the tray will be hot.
Check the meal is piping hot throughout.
Do not reheat once cooled. For other microwave wattages, adjust times according to the manufacturers handbook.

Although extra care has been taken to remove all bones, some may remain.

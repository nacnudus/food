Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: oven

Oven cook from chilled but can also be cooked from frozen. Before cooking: Preheat oven. Remove all packaging. Place pizza directly on the top oven shelf from chilled or middle oven shelf from frozen. Place a baking tray on the shelf below. After cooking: Remove carefully. Check food is piping hot. All cooking appliances vary. This is a guide only. Oven cook from chilled. 220°C. Fan 200°C. Gas 7. 11 mins. Oven cook from frozen. 200°C. Fan 180°C. Gas 6. 19 mins.

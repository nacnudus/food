Information

Description

Free range egg and spinach pasta with a ricotta, basil and pine nut filling

 

These silky green rounds of free-range egg pasta are generously filled with an aromatic blend of Italy's famously sweet and fragrant Genovese basil, creamy ricotta cheese and a sprinkling of pine nuts for added crunch and flavour

Ingredients

Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: other

Cook on the hob from chilled. If freezing at home, defrost thoroughly before cooking. Before cooking: Bring a large pan of water to the boil add the basil & pine nut margherite along with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and salt if preferred. During cooking: Stir gently After cooking: Drain & serve immediately. All cooking appliances vary. This is a guide only. Moderate heat. 4 mins

Weird tasting
I actively disliked this pasta. I was excited to try it as I received a free pack via an online shopping code, and when I initially opened the packet it smelled lovely, strong scent of basil. However all that changed as soon as it hit hot water - the smell was really odd. I cooked it lightly according to the pack, but I didn't enjoy the texture and it had a really bland taste, plus the continuing odd smell. Halfway through eating I gave up and threw it away - the smell was really off-putting and the pasta was flabby. It definitely wasn't gone-off (well within date), but just unpleasant. I have never enjoyed a filled fresh pasta less.

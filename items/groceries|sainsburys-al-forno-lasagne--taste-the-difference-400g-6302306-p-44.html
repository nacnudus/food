Information

Description

Layers of free range egg pasta with minced British beef, British pork and tomato ragù topped with creamy cheese sauce and Parmigiano Reggiano cheese.

 

Free range egg pasta layered with a slow cooked British beef and pork ragù 

Our classic lasagne starts the Italian way, with an aromatic soffrito. We slow cook British beef and British pork, tomatoes, herbs and Chianti to create a rich ragù. Then we layer it with free range egg pasta, creamy béchamel and a sprinkling of parmesan cheese

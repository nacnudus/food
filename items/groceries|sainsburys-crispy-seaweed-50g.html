Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: oven

Oven cook from chilled only. If freezing at home, defrost thoroughly before cooking. This product is not suitable for microwave cooking. Before cooking: Preheat oven. Remove all packaging. Decant contents directly onto a baking tray and place on the middle shelf of the oven. During cooking: Mix halfway through. Continue cooking. After cooking: Check food is piping hot. All cooking appliances vary. This is a guide only. Oven cook from chilled. 200°C. Fan 180°C. Gas 6. 2½ mins.

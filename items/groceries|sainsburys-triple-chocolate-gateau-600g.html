Storage

How to store. bbe: see front of pack. After purchase store and use as below. Defrost thoroughly and use within 24 hours. Do not refreeze after thawing. Food freezer. Until best before date (at -18°C). Star marked frozen food compartment. {3 Stars} Until best before date (at -18°C). {2 Stars} 1 month (at -12°C). {1 Star} 1 week (at -6°C). Ice making compartment. 3 days (at -6°C). Refrigerator. 24 hours.

Once thawed, do not refreeze

DEFROST How to defrost. Remove the gateau from all packaging. Place on a serving plate and leave to defrost at room temperature for 2 - 3 hours. Once defrosted, keep refrigerated and consume within 24 hours.

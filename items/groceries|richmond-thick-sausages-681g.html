Preparation

Why not try...
...stirring in 3-4 tablespoons of your favourite chutney to the baking dish during the last 5 mins of oven cooking for delicious, thick, sticky sausages!

Cooking instructions: General

Please ensure that these sausages are cooked thoroughly before eating. Do not prick Richmond Sausages.

Grill: From Chilled

Set tray on middle shelf. Grill for 15-20 mins on a medium heat, turning occasionally.

Oven cook: From Chilled

Place sausages on a baking tray in the centre of a pre-heated oven at 190°C, 375°F, Gas Mark 5 for 20-25 mins, turning occasionally. For fan assisted ovens adjust cooking times according to manufacturer's handbook.

Shallow fry: From Chilled

Add a little oil to the pan and fry on a medium heat for approx 10-15 mins, turning occasionally.

Although every care has been taken to remove bones, some may remain.

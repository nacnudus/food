Preparation and Safety

Cooking instructions: microwave

Microwave from chilled but can also be heated on the hob. If freezing at home, defrost thoroughly before heating. Before heating: Undo lid and replace loosely. Place on a non-metallic plate. During heating: Stir after 2 minutes. Re-cover and continue heating. After heating: Stir thoroughly. Check food is piping hot. Allow to stand for 1 minute. Do not reheat once cooled. All cooking appliances vary. This is a guide only. Microwave on full power: Cat D 750w: 4 mins Cat E 850w: 3½ mins

 

Cooking instructions: other

To heat on the hob: Empty contents into a saucepan. Moderate heat for 4-5 minutes. Stir continuously until piping hot.

Warnings:

When heating in a microwave the pot may become flexible. Leave on a plate at all times during heating and stirring. Handle pot carefully to avoid scalding.

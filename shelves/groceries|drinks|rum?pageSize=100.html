  Information about alcohol legislation

Please be aware that spirits & liqueurs are no longer eligible for Nectar points. Some restrictions may apply on selected vouchers. For example, when using a money off voucher the value of the goods other than spirits, liqueurs or wine when on a buy 6 save 25% multibuy must be at least equal to the money off offered by the promotional voucher. Other restrictions may apply - see your voucher for details. For details on how this may impact you, please visit the Help Centre.
